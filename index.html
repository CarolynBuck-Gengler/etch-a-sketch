Welcome to Etch A Sketch!

  • You can draw with three different pens: black, random, or one that starts light grey and every time the pen passes over a cell it turns darker.
  • You can also reset the grid (wipe it clean) or reset and choose a different numbers of cells per side.
  • Finally, you can toggle between showing all the grid lines and having an outer border and no grid lines.
